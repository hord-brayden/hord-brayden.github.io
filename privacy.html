Privacy Policy

This is a personal portfolio. The short version: nothing on this page collects, stores, or transmits any personal information about you to me.

Last updated: 2026.

What this site collects

Nothing identifying. Pages are static HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. No analytics, no cookies set by me, no tracking pixels, no server-side logging beyond what GitHub Pages records by default to serve traffic. Settings you toggle on this site (dark mode, your reaction-timer high score) live in your browser via localStorage and never leave your device.

Third-party resources

The site loads a few public CDN resources - fonts (Google Fonts), MathJax (Cloudflare CDN). Loading these resources discloses your IP and User-Agent to those providers, which is standard for any website that uses them. Their privacy policies govern that data.

Cookies

This site does not set cookies. Browser localStorage is used only for the dark-mode preference and the reaction-timer best score. You can clear both at any time from your browser's site-data controls.

Your rights

You have the right to know how data about you is collected and used. Since this site does not collect identifying data about visitors, there is no profile to access, correct, or delete. If you believe that has changed, the best place to flag it is on the site's GitHub repository.
