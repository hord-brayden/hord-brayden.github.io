How it works. Click Start (or press any key) and five red lights come on one at a time, just like an F1 grid. The hold after the fifth light is randomized between 0.8s and 3.2s. When the lights go out, react as fast as you can. Anything under 200 ms is professional-driver territory. Anything that looks under 150 ms is probably a bad sample — humans don't actually go that fast.
