What am I looking at?

  • Tail number: aircraft registration (e.g., N385FX).
  • Flights: browse Flexjet flights (Aviationstack /flights) and pick one to plot origin/destination; if a registration exists we auto-track live.
  • Poll interval: how often we fetch live data.
  • Groundspeed: mph (converted from knots).
  • Altitude: feet (barometric/geom.).
  • Heading: degrees.
  • Last seen: seconds since last ADS-B message.
  • Hex: ICAO transponder address.
  • Squawk: transponder code.

Theme flips between day/night at the aircraft's location. The dotted path is a local trail that grows as you keep the page running.
